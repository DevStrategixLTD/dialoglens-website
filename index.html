How It Works

A Continuous Improvement Engine

Four steps, every month. Each cycle builds on the last, compounding your results over time.

Link your chat platform via API, webhooks, or file upload. We ingest your conversation data securely.

2

Our AI engine processes every conversation — extracting pain points, scoring agents, modeling conversion drivers, and segmenting your users by ICP.

Know Who You're Talking To
